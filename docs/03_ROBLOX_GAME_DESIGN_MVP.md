# 03 — Roblox Game Design MVP

## Working title

**Squishy Smash: The Lost Sparkle**

## Genre

Kid-friendly Roblox collector/simulator/adventure.

## Core loop

Explore a Play Zone, find sleepy squishy friends, tap/click to give them silly squishes, fill their Joy Meter, Happy Pop into sparkles, earn Sparkle Coins, open capsules, discover official character cards, fill the Squishy Book, equip favorite squishies as buddies, and restore Sparkle glimmers.

## MVP scope

Build only the first complete loop in Pudding Hills.

MVP features: spawn area, Pudding Hills starter environment, Soft Dumpling NPC guide, 3–5 placeholder squishy friends, click/tap squish interaction, Joy Meter, Happy Pop reward, Sparkle Coins counter, capsule machine, Collection Book UI, 48-card launch roster, locked/unlocked card state, and local testing through Rojo.

## MVP starter squishies

Use Soft Dumpling, Jelly Bun, Peach Mochi, Cream Puff, Pudding Pop, Sparkle Mochi, and Galaxy Dumpling as first tests.

## Player stats

SparkleCoins, TotalSquishes, TotalHappyPops, CardsDiscovered, CurrentZone, EquippedBuddyId.

## First tutorial quest

Soft Dumpling says the Sparkle feels quiet and asks the player to wake up three sleepy squishies. Player squishes 3, receives 100 Sparkle Coins, opens first capsule, and discovers first card.

## No monetization or trading in MVP

Do not add Game Passes, Developer Products, or trading yet.
