# 09 — Future Roadmap

## After MVP

Add better Pudding Hills art pass, buddy/follower system, Goo Coast, Moonlit Hollow, hidden story pages, daily rewards, decorating a Squishy Room, event capsule support, promo code redemption, and limited-time storybook card frames.

## Storybook tie-ins

Use codes like PMF, SPLOINK, THUP, EVERYSQUISH, and LOSTSPARKLE. Rewards should be small and safe: Sparkle Coins, stickers, card frames, room decorations, and non-paid cosmetics.

## iOS tie-ins

Keep the same character canon across iOS and Roblox: ID, card number, name, pack, rarity, and art where possible. Do not require account linking in the early version.

## Monetization later

Only after the game is fun and stable: Extra Buddy Slots, VIP Cozy Room, Double Sparkle Coins, Lucky Sparkle Capsules, Sparkle Coin packs, temporary luck boost, and cosmetic room bundles.

Keep monetization parent-friendly. Avoid pressure mechanics.

## Physical product tie-ins

Future possibilities: Shopify squishy toys, printed character cards, signed books, giveaway codes, QR codes from book to Roblox experience, and QR codes from toy package to Roblox cosmetic.
