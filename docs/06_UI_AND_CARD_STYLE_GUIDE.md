# 06 — UI and Card Style Guide

The uploaded card images are the official visual reference.

Sample cards included: 001 Soft Dumpling, 002 Jelly Bun, 013 Galaxy Dumpling, 032 Singularity Goo Core, 041 Star-Eyed Bunny, 043 Glow Ghost Puff, 046 Arcane Wobble Kitty, 048 Mythic Plush Familiar.

## Card design observations

Cards use a large readable name, rarity badge, pack badge, large hero art, soft rounded toy-like frame, three stat panels, short lore line, card number, Squishy Smash logo, and strong color/shine differences by rarity.

## Roblox UI direction

For MVP, use full uploaded card images as card art. Do not recreate every card as native Roblox UI first.

## Collection Book layout

Header “Squishy Book”, progress like “12/48 Discovered”, pack tabs, scrollable grid, selected card detail modal, and “Equip Buddy” button.

## Capsule reveal style

Use sparkles, wobble, soft pop, glow, card flip, and gentle sound.

Text examples: “New Friend Discovered!”, “Soft Dumpling joined your Squishy Book!”, “Friendship Bonus!”

## Rarity visual direction

Common soft blue/pink; Rare brighter blue/purple; Epic purple/cosmic glow; Legendary gold frame; Mythic gold + cosmic sparkle.

The physical cards say “Burst Type.” In Roblox native text, prefer “Pop Style.”
