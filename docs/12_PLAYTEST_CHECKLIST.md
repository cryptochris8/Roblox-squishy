# 12 — Playtest Checklist (with the girls)

*The focus group: three daughters, ages 8, 8, and 6. The 6-year-old is the
clarity bar; the 8-year-olds are the "is it boring?" bar. Watch more than you ask —
where they get stuck or light up matters more than what they say.*

## Setup
1. **Publish first** (File → Publish, Alt+P) so the live game has the latest build.
2. Set the experience to **Friends** (Creator Hub → Settings) and make sure each
   girl's Roblox account is your friend, OR Public for the session.
3. Each girl joins on her own device. To be in one server together, one joins,
   the others use **Join** on her in the Roblox friends list.
4. Reset your own profile first if you want to demo from zero (your **🔄 Reset My
   Progress** button — owner-only; the girls can't see it).

## The first 60 seconds (the 6-year-old test)
- [ ] Does she know what to do without being told? (Tap a sleepy friend to squish.)
- [ ] Is the **first goal** clear? (HUD: "Pudding Hills Shard — wake X/8 sleepy friends.")
- [ ] Does talking to **Soft Dumpling** (the guide) make the goal land?
- [ ] Does the **Happy Pop** (sparkles + coins) feel rewarding?
- [ ] Does the **free first capsule** / **🎁 Free Daily Gift** get noticed + opened?

## Each land (do all three)
- [ ] **Pudding Hills** — cozy and readable? The river/bridge/orchard/cottage feel like a place?
- [ ] **Goo Coast** — does it feel *different* (goo sea, pier, sandcastle)? Fun to explore?
- [ ] **Moonlit Hollow** — magical, not scary? (Watch the 6-year-old — it must read "cozy night," never spooky.)
- [ ] Do the **Sparkle Bits** (hidden collectibles) get found? Too easy / too hard?

## The quest + travel
- [ ] Recover a shard (hold E on it once it appears). Is the "shard appeared!" moment clear?
- [ ] After a shard, do they find the **Travel Pad** to the next land?
- [ ] Is it obvious the next land just **opened**? (Toast + the HUD objective updates.)
- [ ] Recover all 3 → the **Restore the Sparkle** celebration. Does it feel like a big finish?

## Collection (the "is it worth coming back?" loop)
- [ ] Do they open the **Squishy Book** and care about filling it? (Completion %, silhouettes.)
- [ ] Do duplicates → **✨Sparkly / 🌈Rainbow** feel exciting (not "a miss")?
- [ ] Do **daily quests** + the **🔥 streak** make them want to return tomorrow?

## Boredom / difficulty read (the 8-year-olds)
- [ ] Where does interest dip? (Note the exact moment.)
- [ ] Too easy anywhere? Too grindy (waking N friends per shard)?
- [ ] What did they want to do that they couldn't? (Wishlist for Phase C/social.)

## Known limitations — expected, don't treat as bugs
- **40 of 48 cards show a colored placeholder** (only 8 have real art yet).
- Friends + buddies are **placeholder squishy balls with faces** (no 3D meshes yet).
- **No co-op events / leaderboards yet** (Phase C).
- **No purchases** (by design — capsules are free).
- Distant lands take a second to **stream in** after you travel (normal).

## Questions to ask each girl afterward
- What was your favorite land, and why?
- Which Squishy Friend do you wish you had?
- What was confusing or boring?
- Would you play it again tomorrow? What would make you want to?
